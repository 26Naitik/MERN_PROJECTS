Story Line

Interstellar is a science fiction movie directed by Christopher Nolan. The movie follows astronauts who travel through space using a wormhole to find a new planet for humanity because Earth is becoming unlivable.

The movie explores black holes, time travel, emotions, sacrifice, and survival of mankind.
